'Monumento à
J-u-s-t-i-ça'

Being a monument to justice itself, this prototype-versionof the model is intended to demonstrate a bare-bones version of justice.
Stripped of color and ornament, this monument declares itself as the foundation to one's vision of justice.
In the final version, it is envisioned to morph itself into the user's personal interpretation of justice.
For now, simply place Monumento à J-u-s-t-i-ça on the ground!
